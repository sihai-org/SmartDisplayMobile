How to request account deletion (prominent)

In-app profile (recommended): Open VznGPT → tap Profile → Settings → Account & Security → tap Delete Account and complete confirmation.

Important: Deletion is irreversible and will disable services tied to the account, including saved content, preferences, and cloud backups.
